Remove Duplicates FAQ

        

            

                How does duplicate detection work?
                

                    
The tool compares rows based on selected columns. By default, it checks all columns - rows that match on all columns are considered duplicates.

                

            

            

                What data formats are supported?
                

                    
Supports CSV format with various delimiters (comma, semicolon, tab, pipe). JSON arrays are also supported.

                

            

            

                Is my data uploaded to a server?
                

                    
No. All processing happens in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.
