Cookie Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you use the site, improving your browsing experience.

2. Cookies We Use

AI JSON uses minimal cookies to ensure the experience. Here's a breakdown:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration Required?
Essential Cookies Core functionality like navigation and security Session Yes
Analytics Cookies Understand how visitors use our site (anonymized) 30 days No
Preference Cookies Remember your settings (like dark/light mode) 1 year No

3. How We Use Cookies

AI JSON is designed to minimize cookie usage. Since all our JSON utilities process data locally in your browser, we don't need to store your data on our servers. We use cookies only for:

  • Website Functionality: Ensuring the site works properly
  • Analytics: Understanding which tools are popular (privacy-friendly, anonymized)
  • Your Preferences: Remembering your display preferences

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party services to set cookies on our site for:

  • Analytics Providers: To help us understand site usage (always anonymized)
  • Content Delivery Networks: For faster page loading

These third parties have their own privacy policies governing their cookie usage.

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options for managing cookies:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through settings
  • Opt-Out Links: Analytics providers typically offer opt-out options
  • Cookie Consent Banner: On your first visit, you can choose to accept or decline non-essential cookies

6. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable cookies:

  • Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are required for the site to function
  • Analytics cookies: Disabling may limit our ability to improve the site
  • Preference cookies: Your settings won't be saved between visits

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at aijsons.com or refer to our Privacy Policy.
