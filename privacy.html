AI JSON is designed with your privacy in mind. Our online JSON tools process data entirely within your browser. We do not collect, store, transmit, or log any JSON data you process on our website.

We use collected information solely for:

3. Data Security

Since all JSON processing happens locally in your browser, your data never leaves your device. This means:
