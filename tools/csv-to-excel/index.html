CSV to Excel FAQ

        

            

                How to convert CSV to Excel?
                

                    
Simply paste your CSV data, choose your delimiter, and click "Convert to XLSX". The file will download automatically.

                

            

            

                What is the difference between CSV and Excel?
                

                    
CSV is a plain text format with comma-separated values. Excel (XLSX) is a spreadsheet format that supports formulas, multiple sheets, and formatting.

                

            

            

                Is my data uploaded to a server?
                

                    
No. All processing happens in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.
